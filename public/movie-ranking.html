The Best Movies according to Hazel

My top 3 movies of all time

The Fault in Our Stars

The Fault in Our Stars by John Green is a young adult fiction novel that narrates the story of a 16-year-old girl who is diagnosed with cancer. She joins a support group where she meets Augustus, and there is a rollercoaster of emotions throughout this novel as the relationship between Hazel and Augustus develops.

Love, Rosie

Based on the bestselling novel and from the writer of PS I Love You, Love, Rosie is a heart-warming, modern and romantic comedy-of-errors. Since the age 5, Rosie and Alex have been best friends, facing the highs and lows of growing up side by side. A fleeting shared moment, one missed opportunity, and the decisions that follow send their lives in completely different directions

The Secret Life of Pets

Continuing the story of Max and his pet friends, following their secret lives after their owners leave them for work or school each day.
